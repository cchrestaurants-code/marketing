User Management

Users and passwords are managed directly in your Google Sheet → Users tab. To add, remove or change a user's password:

1. Open your Google Sheet
2. Click the Users tab
3. Edit the username, password, or role columns
4. Set active to yes or no

Roles: admin — full access & settings  |  editor — add/edit entries  |  viewer — read only
